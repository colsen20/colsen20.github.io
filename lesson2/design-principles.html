Design Principles

by Charles Olsen

Hick's Law

Company: Tesla

https://www.tesla.com/

The Tesla website follows Hick's Law because because you are presented with an imediant call to action, without too many choices. It makes it easy and quick to choose an option. You are immediantly asked to start an order, or to learn more.

Visual hierarchy

Company: Barefut

https://barefut.com/

I think that Barefut follows Visual hierarchy because the test "The World's Finest Essential Oils" immediantly stands out.

White Space and Clean Design

Company: Dag Pawty

https://www.dogpawty.com/

Dog Pawty is super simple, its a basic white background with some purple. They don't overcomplicate things.
