SCENARIOS

Scenario #1

James Bell is 13 years old, he is planning a day to play baseball with his friends. Smart Weather is usefull for this, he can check what day would have good weather for baseball, he can also choose to recieve notifications on when a good day for baseball is coming up.

Benji Eggleston is in college, he wants to know what time of day he should choose for one of his classes. It takes him about 20 minutes to walk. Smart Weather can be usefull in determaining what time of day might on average will have the most comfertable weather for walking to class.

Scenario #3

Phillip Olsen is a small buisness owner. He is planning a meeting with a client. He wants to make sure he chooses a good time and place to meet. Smart Weather can help ensure Phillip, that he is meeting on a day with clear skies.
